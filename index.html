What is SheHacks?

SheHacks is a non profitable, student held hackathon to introduce young girls across India to coding through the experience of hackathon. With women vastly underrepresented in tech industry all over the world, Hacks strives to inspire a new wave of Indian female coders to bring equality to the booming industry.

With women gearing towards technology, we at SheHacks feel it is the need of the hour to provide them with as much motivation as possible. To incorporate the same into the event, we have female developers who guide and mentor all participants throughout the hackathon and conduct workshops in Python/Django and give motivational tech talks.You can check our Code of Conduct.

FAQS

Who can attend?

If you're a student and identify as female, we'd love to have you at SheHacks! Whether that's high school, undergrad, or graduate, we believe you can get something out of the event.

How big are teams?

Teams can consist of anywhere from 2-3 people.

What should I bring?

You'll require any kind of photo ID for check-in, in addition to your usual hacking gear like laptops, chargers.You can find fast internet facilities as well as appropriate accomodation on campus :)

How much does SheHacks cost?

Nothing! We'll also have food, snacks, and prizes.

Can I work on existing projects?

Nope, you can't.The projects have to be developed within the given timeframe from scratch.

Do you offer travel reimbursements?

Yes, we can! We will review it on a case-to-case basis upto a certain cap.

What if I get tired?

Sleep xD We will have matresses available for you rest!

Where is SheHacks?

SheHacks will be held at CC3, Indian Institute Of Information Technology, Allahabad in Prayagraj, India.
